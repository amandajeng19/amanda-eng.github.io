Part 1: Overview of the Dataset

The featured dataset includes video game sales data across various regions, genres, and publishers from 1980 to 2020.

1.1 Name

The title of the video game. This dataset lists 16,598 video games.

Type: Nominal

1.2 Platform

The gaming platform on which the game was released (e.g. Wii, NES).

The platforms with the most games sold were DS (2,163) and PS2 (2,161). The platforms with the fewest games sold were GG (1), PCFX (1), 3DO (3), SCD (6),

Type: Nominal/Categorical

1.3 Year

The year the game was released. The release dates span from 1980–2020.

Also, 271 games are listed as "N/A" for year of release. For this reason, these games will not be included in any year-based analyses.

Type: Ordinal/Categorical

1.4 Genre

The genre of the video game (e.g. Sports, Platform, Racing).

Type: Nominal/Categorical

1.5 Publisher

The publisher of the game (e.g. Nintendo).

Type: Nominal/Categorical

1.6 Sales in North America

Total sales in the North America region (in millions of units).

Type: Numerical/Quantitative

1.7 Sales in Europe

Total sales in the Europe region (in millions of units).

Type: Numerical/Quantitative

1.8 Sales in Japan

Total sales in Japan (in millions of units).

Type: Numerical/Quantitative

1.9 Sales in Other Regions

Total sales in other regions (in millions of units).

Type: Numerical/Quantitative

1.10 Global Sales

Total global sales (in millions of units).

Type: Numerical/Quantitative
